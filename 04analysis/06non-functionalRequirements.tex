\subsubsection{Non-functional requirements} \label{sec:analysis_Non-functional}
Non-functional requirements describe what should a system be and how should it perform. They can be evaluated based on measures. User usually cannot directly see their implementation but can rather feel it by the performance. Often the architecture of the system is influenced by them.
